Biratnagar Traffic Chowk Trek

Trekking in those restricted trekking routes must need a special permit. You need to have a guide, a local guide. Trekking in restricted region is not hard to navigate the trail yourself in all favorable condition but the remoteness has made this trekking kind of tough so is in hand of nature. If weather of Nepali mountain in not in favor – visitors might lose their life in the wilderness. Looking at those worse scenarios and less accessible without proper trekking or tour equipment – these trekking destinations of Nepal are marked as restricted regions.Among dozens of restricted trekking trail few of them are alike to Everest base camp trekking or Annapurna base camp trekking route.

But few other are completely different. Upper Mustang, Dolpo region, Narphu valley trekking are rain shadow trekking zone and interestingly dry in comparison to the rest of Nepali mountains. That is why these routes are famous as rain shadow trekking destination in Nepal as well. Restricted trekking trail are decorated with the mountains on one hand and amazing cultures of the locals is interesting. These restricted regions are either tea house trekking destinations or mixed with tea house trekking or camping trekking. With this being said, these routes are less trekked destination with very fewer tourist in the region. Nothing like of Everest basecamp trekking route lineup or crowd of Annapurna base camp trekking can be seen in these restricted trekking destinations of Nepal.Trekking in these remote destinations is surely basic in accommodation and food but the untouched beauty of nature is luring.

  • Day 02: Full day sightseeing tour in Kathmandu at places of interest.

    In Kathmandu where our city guide takes you around for short drive and walk to visit interesting old monuments of religious and historical background around Old Durbar Square (palace courtyard), ancient Buddhist Stupa of Swayambhunath, Bouddhanath with Hindu temple of Pashupatinath, after an enjoyable and fascinating tour head back to hotel with time for trek preparation leaving early morning for Pokhara.

Good to Know

  • A warm sleeping bag rated for temperatures up to -15 degree Celsius.
  • Comfortable hiking boots. Make sure they are tried and tested before you go on this trek. Carry spare boot laces and lightweight trainers/shoes as well.
  • A waterproof jacket a fleece jacket and a down jacket. Also carry a Fleece sweater.
